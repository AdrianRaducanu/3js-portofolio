Fireflies behavior

Fireflies offer a natural source of light, helping you see better and locate objectives faster. Summon them for a quicker search.

On nighttime, summon fireflies from anywhere to follow you across the entire map.

Fireflies move exclusively within the cave during the daytime.

Changing the scenario from night to day while outside the cave causes the fireflies to relocate to their initial position.

Controls

A - rotate on left

D - rotate on right

S - make a flip and call fireflies

About this game

The volcano has erupted! To save the World, you must unlock all the objectives! Best of luck!

Unlock objectives by simply getting close and looking towards to them.

Once the World is rescued, the default scenario will be linked to the weather and time of day at your location.

Weather is based on open-meteo API and the night is between 7pm and 7am.

If you choose not to disclose your location, you'll be placed into a random scenario.
